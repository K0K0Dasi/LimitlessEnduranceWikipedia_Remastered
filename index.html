Limitless Endurance: Wikipedia

A Discord Motorsport Roleplay Server

Limitless: Endurance Remastered is a motorsport based roleplay server based off the FIA World Endurance Championship, taking elements off of WEC and IMSA to create a Fantasy based Roleplay. The server is connected to its Formula One counterpart Future Titians A New Beginning, and both work side by side. The Inaugrial season of Limitless Endurance was 2027, one year after the Future Titans in 2026.


Litmitless: Endurance is currently under the Hypercar regulations (Le Mans Hybrid and Le Mans Daytona Hybrid), aswell as LMGT3 for the 2030 season of the FIA World Endurance Championship.
